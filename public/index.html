About Us

Welcome to our Telugu Samskruthika Samithi-IIT Kanpur, where people gather to celebrate festivals, enjoy cultural activities, and savor traditional Telugu cuisine.

Celebrate Our Festivals

  • Celebrate Telugu festivals like Ugadi, Sankranti, and Diwali with us. Our events include traditional rituals, community gatherings, and special festivities that bring these vibrant occasions to life.
  • Enjoy a variety of cultural performances that highlight Telugu traditions. From music and dance to drama, our shows offer a glimpse into our rich cultural heritage.
  • Experience the depth of Telugu heritage through our activities. Our events provide opportunities to connect with our traditions and celebrate our cultural values in a meaningful way.

Engage in Cultural Activities

  • Our community is dedicated to preserving and promoting Telugu culture through various activities.
  • We organize performances that highlight our cultural traditions and celebrate our heritage.
  • These events provide opportunities for members to experience and connect with our rich cultural history.

Savor Telugu Cuisine

  • Savor a variety of traditional Telugu recipes, including spicy curries, flavorful rice dishes, and delectable sweets.
  • Experience dishes prepared with age-old recipes and local ingredients, reflecting the rich culinary heritage of Telugu cuisine.
  • Enjoy a delicious and immersive experience that brings the unique flavors of Telugu food to life at our food events.
