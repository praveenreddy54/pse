Director Power

Soon after the engineering of Stage-I Units (3 x 210 MW) for Second Thermal Power Station was completed, the department was formed in 1983 and was named as Power Station Planning (PSP), Sri.S.Srinivasa Raghavan was the first head of this department. In 1984 the department was expanded as Corporate Planning with three divisions l.e. Corporate Planning, Power Station Planning and Mine Planning Sri.N.Venkatesan was the first head of this department. The Corporate Planning department took care of all works from Conceptualization of the project upto handing over to the construction team, including getting clearances from Government and various other agencies, basic engineering and detailed engineering In the 1980's many new power projects were planned by NLC and hence the need for a separate department exclusively for engineering of New Power Projects arose.

Hence the Corporate Planning department was split as Corporate Planning which was subsequently renamed as New Projects and presently named as Projects & Business Development (P&BD) department. Power Station Planning which was renamed as Power Station Engineering (PSE) in the year 2003. P&BD department takes care of all works upto getting government clearances. PSE department takes care of engineering of New Power Projects from concept of commisioning.

Major Tasks Being Undertaken By PSE

  • Technical Review of Feasibility Report.
  • Review of Projects Estimates.
  • Fixing Engineering Consultant for Power Projects.
  • Review & Finalization of concept note for various packages.
  • Preparation of OR for various projects.
  • Review & Finalization of Tender specification for Projects/Packages.
  • Techno-commercial Evaluation of Tenders for Projects/Packages.
  • Price Evaluation for Projects/Packages.
  • Review and Approval of Package Contract drawings and documents.
  • Review and Approval of Quality Assurance Plans.
  • Co-ordination with the Project site till completion of the Final Take Over.
